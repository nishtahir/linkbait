What is Linkbait?

Linkbait is a bot framework that's awesome. It's got a whole bunch of neat features, but most importantly, it's super extensible with support for plugins. It's a really easy way to super charge your Slack or Discord

What does it do?

Pretty much everything. It's got gifs, delicious nutella, unfurls links from app stores, steam and even subreddit mentions, a meme generator and much much more.

Did I mention that it has a Pokedex?


What platforms are supported?

It currently supports Slack and Discord, but we are working to support new clients everyday.


How much is all this awesomeness going to cost me?

Nothing it's free and open source.
