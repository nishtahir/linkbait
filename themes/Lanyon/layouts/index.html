What is Linkbait?

Linkbait is a bot framework that's awesome. It's got a whole bunch of neat features, but most importantly, it's super extensible with support for plugins; It comes with a whole bunch of plugins from gifs to a pokedex.

It currently supports Slack and Discord, but we are working to support new clients everyday.
